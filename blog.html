  • The Importance of Safe Medication Use

    Taking medications correctly is essential for maintaining good health. Improper use of medicines can lead to side effects or serious health problems. Always follow instructions from your pharmacist or doctor.

    Tips for Safe Medication Use:

    No. Tip
    1 Take medications exactly as prescribed.
    2 Do not exceed the recommended dose.
    3 Store medicines in a safe place, away from children.
    4 Check expiration dates before use.
    5 Consult your pharmacist if you have any questions.

    Pharmacists play a key role in educating patients. Consulting them helps prevent side effects and ensures safe, effective treatment.
